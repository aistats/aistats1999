--- title: Accommodation layout: default --- Hotel
The meetings will be held at the Radisson Bahia Mar Hotel in Fort Lauderdale, Florida, January 3-6, 1999.

Making Reservations
To make a reservation, call (954) 764-2233 or (800) 333 3333 and let the receptionist know you are with the Society for Artificial Intelligence and Statistics. You should receive a special group rate of $129/night (single or double). We have reserved a block of 65 rooms, which will be held until December 21, 1998. So make your reservations early!

Visitor Parking
As you enter the parking garage, tell the attendant that you will be attending the "Uncertainty '99". They will have the event keyed into the register, and will bill the Department directly for the parking fee. (However, should something with this procedure go wrong, pay the parking fee of $__ and we will reimburse you.)
